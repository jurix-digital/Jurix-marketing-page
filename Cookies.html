This Cookie Policy explains how Jurix uses cookies and similar technologies to recognise you, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

01What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help the service function, remember your choices, and understand how the platform is used.

02Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential — required for sign-in, security and core functionality. These cannot be switched off.
  • Functional — remember preferences such as language and saved filters.
  • Analytics — help us understand usage so we can improve the platform.
  • Marketing — used sparingly to measure the effectiveness of our campaigns.

03Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and adjust in-app preferences where available. Blocking essential cookies may affect core features such as staying signed in. You can also review and change your choices any time using our cookie-consent banner — open cookie preferences.

04Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by trusted third parties (for example, analytics and payment providers) to deliver their services. These providers are evaluated for security and privacy as part of our vendor due diligence.

05Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent revision.

Questions about this policy?

You're always in control of your cookie preferences. Reach our team at legal@jurix.in or visit our Contact page.
