Security & Trust

How we protect your data and verify professionals.

We use AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, role-based access controls and tamper-evident audit logs. Full detail is on the Trust & Security page.

Lawyers complete KYC and bar council verification before taking cases, and we periodically re-verify credentials and monitor for disciplinary actions.

Customer data is stored in Indian data centers in line with India's data-residency requirements and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
